ARtPUT: Autonomous Rover to Pick Up Trash

Building a sustainable future through automation: an autonomous rover designed to clean our planet, one piece of trash at a time.

Welcome to ARtPUT

ARtPUT is a robotics initiative committed to leveraging autonomous technology to improve environmental cleanliness. Our rover detects, collects, and removes trash in outdoor spaces, showcasing how engineering can make a meaningful ecological impact.
