ARtPUT: Autonomous Rover to Pick Up Trash

Innovating sustainable solutions through robotics — combining automation and environmental care to make our world cleaner.

Welcome to ARtPUT

ARtPUT is a robotics project dedicated to developing an autonomous rover that can identify, collect, and dispose of litter in public environments. Our goal is to improve environmental cleanliness and reduce human workload in repetitive or hazardous cleanup operations.
